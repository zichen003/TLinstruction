Summary


Earnings: The mover who occupies the position, the occupant, earns 103 points at the end of Stage 3.
The mover who does not occupy the position, the non-occupant, earns 63 points at the end of Stage 3.

If Mover B has occupied the position, and Mover A chooses not to reduce Mover B's points:
Mover B, the occupant, earns 103 - 0 = 103 points at the end of Stage 3;
Mover A, the non-occupant, earns 63 - 0 = 63 points at the end of Stage 3.

If Mover B has occupied the position, and Mover A chooses to reduce Mover B's points:
Mover B, the occupant, earns 103 - 50 = 53 points at the end of Stage 3;
Mover A, the non-occupant, earns 63 - 10 = 53 points at the end of Stage 3.
